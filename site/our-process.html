Our Approach

From design to installation

We handle every step of your countertop project with precision, professionalism, and attention to detail.

Start Your Project
Step 1

Design Consultation

We start with a free in-home or showroom consultation. Bring your cabinet drawings, photos, budget, and timeline. We'll discuss material options, edge profiles, finishes, and placement to make sure every detail matches your vision.

  • Site assessment & measurements
  • Material & finish recommendations
  • Detailed project timeline
  • Transparent pricing & quotes
Step 2

Digital Templating & Fabrication

Once you approve the design, we create a precise digital template using ProLiner technology. Our CNC equipment cuts your stone to exact specifications, and our skilled craftspeople hand-finish every edge for a luxury feel and perfect fit.

  • ProLiner digital templating
  • Precision CNC cutting
  • Hand-polished edges
  • Quality inspection before delivery
Step 3

Professional Installation

Our in-house installation crews protect your home, properly remove old countertops, set and level your new surfaces, and seal/polish everything to perfection. We leave no mess behind and do a detailed walk-through inspection.

  • Professional old top removal
  • Precise leveling & setting
  • Final sealing & polishing
  • Detailed punch-list review

Timeline

Most projects take 3-4 weeks from consultation to installation, depending on material availability and your schedule. Complex projects or custom materials may take longer. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your initial consultation.
