Quartz

Engineered quartz countertops

Quartz combines natural stone with resins for a consistent, low-maintenance surface that never needs sealing.

  • ✓Excellent for busy kitchens, baths and rentals
  • ✓Wide range of whites, marbles, concretes and solids
  • ✓Resistant to most stains with simple day-to-day care

Best for

  • Clean, modern designs
  • Low-maintenance households
  • Consistent patterns across large islands
