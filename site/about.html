Our Story

Family-owned stone & glass experts

For over 20 years, Olive Glass & Marble has been serving homeowners, builders, and designers with the highest quality countertops and glass installations in North Carolina and beyond.

Our Company

Olive Glass & Marble was founded with a simple mission: deliver exceptional craftsmanship, premium materials, and outstanding customer service. What started as a small operation has grown into one of the region's most trusted countertop and glass fabricators.

We believe in doing business the right way—with integrity, transparency, and a genuine commitment to customer satisfaction. Every project, whether it's a small residential bathroom or a large commercial installation, receives our full attention and expertise.

Our in-house team of designers, fabricators, and installers work together seamlessly to ensure consistency, quality, and accountability at every step. We're not outsourcing—we're controlling the entire process from consultation to completion.
