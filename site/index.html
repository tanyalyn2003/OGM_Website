Our Services

From design to installation

We handle every step of your project in-house—design consultation, precision fabrication, and professional installation.

01 · Design Consultation

Material selection & planning

Bring your cabinet drawings, photos, or ideas. We'll help you choose the perfect stone, finishes, and edge profiles that match your vision and budget.

  • Granite, marble, quartz & specialty stones
  • Edge profiles and finishing options
  • Sink and appliance coordination
02 · Precision Fabrication

CNC-cut, hand-finished

Using state-of-the-art digital templating and precision cutting equipment, combined with hand finishing for a luxury feel. Every seam is planned for strength and beauty.

  • Digital ProLiner templating
  • Precision CNC cutting
  • Hand-polished finishes
03 · Professional Installation

Clean, on-time, respectful

Our in-house crews protect your home, properly set and level every surface, and leave everything polished and sealed. We work around your schedule.

  • Old countertop removal & disposal
  • Final sealing & polish
  • Detailed walk-through inspection
