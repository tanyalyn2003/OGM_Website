Our Services

From Design to Installation

Every successful countertop project starts with the right process — and at Olive Glass & Marble, we handle it all under one roof. From the moment you walk into our showroom to the final polish on your new surface, our in-house team manages the entire journey.

With over two decades of experience, we've refined a process that ensures quality, efficiency, and clear communication at every phase. You'll work with knowledgeable staff who guide you through design decisions, skilled fabricators who cut every slab with precision, and professional installers who treat your home with care.

Whether you're remodeling your kitchen, refreshing a bathroom, or building a new space, we make the experience straightforward, collaborative, and results-driven — from first sketch to final seal.

01 · Design Consultation

Material selection & planning

Bring your cabinet drawings, inspiration photos, or design ideas — we'll help you choose the perfect slab and finishes to match your vision. From edge details to sink selections, we ensure your space is ready for its new look.

  • Granite, marble, quartz & more
  • Edge profiles and finishing options
  • Sink and appliance coordination
02 · Precision Fabrication

CNC-cut, hand-finished

Using state-of-the-art digital templating and precision cutting equipment, combined with hand finishing for a luxury feel and high-end result. Every seam is carefully planned for strength, fit, and lasting beauty.

  • Digital ProLiner templating
  • Precision CNC cutting
  • Hand-polished finishes
03 · Professional Installation

Clean, on-time, respectful

Our in-house crews protect your home, properly set and level every surface, and leave everything polished, sealed, and ready to impress. We do our best to accommodate your schedule and keep the process smooth from start to finish.

  • Old countertop removal & disposal
  • Final sealing & polish
  • Detailed walk-through inspection
Material Options

Premium Stones for Every Style

From bold statement pieces to timeless classics, we offer a wide selection of materials to match any design vision. Choose from full slabs or cost-effective remnant pieces — perfect for kitchens, bathrooms, bars, and more.

Our curated inventory includes granite, marble, quartz, and quartzite, along with a selection of specialty stones. Whether you're drawn to the natural movement of granite, the soft elegance of marble, or the low-maintenance appeal of engineered quartz, we'll help you find the ideal surface for your space.

Our expert team is here to guide you through material comparisons, edge styles, and finish options — ensuring the stone you select aligns with your aesthetic, functionality needs, and budget.

Granite — Bianco Antico

A durable and timeless choice with unique patterning and natural speckling. Granite is heat-resistant and ideal for kitchens, bathrooms, and outdoor use. Bianco Antico blends well in both traditional and transitional styles.

Quartzite — Taj Mahal

A natural stone known for its elegant veining and creamy tones. Quartzite is harder than granite, offering superior resistance to heat, scratches, and daily wear — perfect for high-end kitchens and bathrooms.

Marble — Carrara White

Classic and refined, Carrara marble brings timeless beauty with soft veining and cool tones. Ideal for vanities, islands, and feature walls where elegance and character are key. Requires sealing and care to preserve its finish.
